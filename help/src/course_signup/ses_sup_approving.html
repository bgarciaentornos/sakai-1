Approving and Rejecting Students on a Module for Supervisors

If you are a Supervisor you are able to approve and reject the requests from students under your supervision who have enrolled on a module. You will receive an email when any of your students enrol for a module asking you to approve or reject the request. You will need to log into WebLearn using your single sign-on user name and password to complete the process.

  1. When you receive an automated email, click on the link provided in the email which will take you into the SES.
  2. When a student has been Accepted by the Module Administrator they will appear in the Pending Acceptances screen in the WebLearn interface. Using the hyperlinks under Actions by each student's name you can change it to Approve or Reject.
  3. By clicking Approve the student will be sent an email informing them that they have been approved and are able to attend the course. By clicking Reject they will be sent an email informing them that they have been rejected and are unable to attend the course.

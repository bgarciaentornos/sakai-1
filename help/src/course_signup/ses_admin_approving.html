Approving a Student to Attend a Module

If you are a Supervisor and have emailed with a request to approve a student to attend a Module, see Approving a Student to Attend a Module as a Supervisor . If you are a Module Administrator and are required to approve a student, see Approving a Student to Attend a Module as a Module Administrator.

Approving a Student to Attend a Module as a Supervisor
  1. Click on Pending Acceptances at the top of the page.
  2. A list of pending acceptances is shown. The right-most column are the hyperlinks Accept or Reject.

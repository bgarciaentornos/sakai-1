Signing Up for a Module

Once you have found a Module you wish to attend, carefully check you fulfill the eligibility criteria to make sure the course is suitable for you.

Note: If the Module is marked Full see Students: What to do if a Module is Full.

  1. Select the Module Parts you wish attend - this will be made clear in the Module Description (eg you may be required to sign up for both a Laboratory and Lecture series). Click the Signup button at the bottom of the course description.

  2. A pop up window will ask for your supervisor's email address and your reasons for wanting to attend the Module. This is needed because your supervisor's permission is required for you to attend any training Modules.

    Note:You will need to enter an email address recognised by the University.

  3. Following submission an email will be sent to your Supervisor and the Module Administrator for approval.

  4. When approval has been granted you will be notified via email that you have a place on the Module.

    You will also be notified if approval is not granted.

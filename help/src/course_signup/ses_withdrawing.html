Withdrawing from a Module

Check the status of your application by clicking on My Modules and following the corresponding instructions below.

If your status on the Module is PENDING

  1. Click on My Modules at the top of the page.
  2. In the status column next to the Module you wish to withdraw from will be link labelled 'Withdraw'. By selecting this you will be WITHDRAWN.
  3. An email will be sent to both the Module Administrator and your Supervisor to notify them of your withdrawal.

If your status on the Module is APPROVED

  1. If you have been APPROVED on a Module you will need to email the Module Administrator in order to withdraw. The details of the Module Administrator are found on the acceptance email you received.

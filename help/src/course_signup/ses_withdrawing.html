Withdrawing from a Module

Check the status of your application (refer to Students: Checking My Status on a Module). If your status on the Module is PENDING see Withdrawing from a Module if your application is PENDING, otherwise see Withdrawing from a Module if your application is APPROVED.

Withdrawing from a Module if your application is PENDING
  1. Click on My Modules at the top of the page.
  2. Next to the Module you wish to withdraw from will be a dropdown box. Select WITHDRAWN.
  3. An email will be sent to both the Module Administrator and your Supervisor to notify them of your withdrawal.

Withdrawing from a Module if your application is APPROVED

If you have been APPROVED on a Module you will need to email the Module Administrator in order to withdraw. The details of the Module Administrator are found on the acceptance email you received.
